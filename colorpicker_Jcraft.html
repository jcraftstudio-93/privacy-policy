Privacy Policy — ColorPicker

Effective date: 2025-10-13 · Developer: JCraft Studio

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the ColorPicker app (“the App”, “we”) handles your information. The current version of the App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All features operate entirely on your device, and we do not transmit data to our servers or to third parties.

Data Collection & Processing

  • Personal data: Not collected.
  • Usage analytics: Not collected.
  • Tracking technologies (e.g., cookies, SDK beacons): Not used.
  • Account or login: Not required.

Permissions

The App does not request sensitive permissions (such as Contacts, Location, or Photos/Media) in its current version. If future features require permissions, they will be requested transparently and used only for the stated purpose.

Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

The current version shows no ads and includes no advertising SDKs. If we add ads (e.g., Google AdMob) in a future update, this Policy and the Google Play “Data safety” section will be updated before release.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age as defined by local law) and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” will be revised accordingly. Material changes will be highlighted in the update notes and/or within the App.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at jcraftstudio111@gmail.com.

Note for reviewers: This is a “no data collected” build. No analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs related to data processing are included.
