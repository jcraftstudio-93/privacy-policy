1. App Overview

Ladder Game Random Picker is a simple utility app for creating random ladder game results. Users can enter names and results, generate a ladder, view the result, and check recent result history.

This app is not a gambling, betting, casino, lottery, or real-money reward app. It is intended only for casual random selection and entertainment utility purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect Directly

The app does not require account registration and does not ask users to enter directly identifying personal information such as name, email address, phone number, address, government ID, or payment information.

The names or result labels entered by users for the ladder game are used only for app functionality. They are processed on the device and are not intentionally transmitted to the developer's server.
