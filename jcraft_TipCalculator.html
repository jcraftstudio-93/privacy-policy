Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the application collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Information Collection

This app does not collect or store personal information directly.

The app may use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob for advertising.

AdMob may collect certain information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location information
  • Usage data

This information may be used to provide and improve advertising services.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Data Usage

Any information collected by third-party services may be used for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Improving service performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

Data Security

The app does not store personal information directly. Third-party services handle any data according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

Developer Information

For developer contact information, please refer to the developer information listed on the Google Play Store page of this application.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
