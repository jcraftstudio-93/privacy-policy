1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how JCraft Studio (“Developer”) handles information in Annual Leave Calculator (“App”).

The App primarily stores leave records and notes locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not require an account and does not upload your leave data to our servers.

  • Granted days, leave records (annual/half-day/special), date notes, language settings: stored on-device only

The App may include AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google and its partners may collect/process certain information to serve ads.

  • Advertising identifier (e.g., Android Advertising ID), IP address, device/OS information, approximate location (inferred), ad interaction/performance data

Data collection and processing depend on ad settings and consent options where applicable.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features: leave calculation, logs, calendar view, notes (local storage)
  • Ads & measurement: show ads to keep the App free, frequency control, performance measurement, fraud prevention

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or rent your on-device leave data. However, the App may use Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) for advertising.

  • Third Party: Google LLC (AdMob / Google Mobile Ads)

For more information, please review Google’s privacy policies (search: “Google Privacy Policy”, “AdMob Privacy”).

8. Your Choices
