Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application Countdown Board provided by JCraft Studio.

For developer contact information, please refer to the developer information listed on the official Google Play Store page.

1. Information Collection

This application is designed to operate primarily on the user's device.

The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts

All countdown events created by the user are stored locally on the device and are not transmitted to external servers.

2. Local Data Storage

The application may store certain information locally on the user's device in order to provide core functionality, including:

  • Countdown event information
  • Display preferences
  • Language settings
  • Application configuration

This information remains on the user's device and is not shared externally by the application.

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify users.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Third-party services used by the app may include:

  • Google AdMob (advertising service)

These services may collect certain device identifiers, usage data, or advertising identifiers according to their own privacy policies.

Please refer to the privacy policy of these providers for more details.

4. Advertising

This application may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks.

Advertising providers may collect and use certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • App interaction data

This data is used for ad delivery, ad performance measurement, and improving advertising services.

5. Children's Privacy

This application is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer through the information provided on the app's Google Play page.

6. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
