Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Poker Hand Guide handles information when you use the app. The app is provided by JCraft Studio.

Poker Hand Guide is a rule reference and hand comparison tool for poker hands. It does not provide real-money betting, gambling, casino services, cash rewards, or win prediction services.

1. Information We Collect

Poker Hand Guide does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to enter personal information such as name, email address, phone number, address, or payment information.

The app may store app-related data locally on your device, such as selected language, theme setting, and recent comparison history. This data is used only to provide app features and remains on your device unless you delete the app data or uninstall the app.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect and process certain information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and diagnostic information in accordance with Google's policies.

This information may be used by Google and its partners to provide, measure, and improve advertising services, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on user settings, consent, region, and applicable law.

For more information, please review Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policy

3. Local App Data

The app may save recent hand comparison records and app preferences locally on your device. These records are not uploaded to a server operated by JCraft Studio.

You can delete locally stored app data by using the app's delete functions where available, clearing app data through your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

4. Data Sharing

JCraft Studio does not sell personal information. The app does not directly transmit your poker hand selections or comparison history to JCraft Studio.

However, third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob may collect and process data as described in their own privacy policies.

5. Children

This app is not directed to children. The app is intended as a general rule reference and comparison utility. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

6. No Gambling or Betting Services

Poker Hand Guide does not provide real-money betting, gambling, casino services, cash rewards, or any service that allows users to wager money or items of value.

The app is intended only for rule reference, hand ranking review, and comparison of selected card combinations.

7. Security

We use reasonable efforts to keep the app safe and reliable. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. Users are responsible for managing their own device security and app data.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Developer Information and Contact

Developer information and contact details are available on the Google Play Store listing for this app.
