소개

권한

앱은 기능 제공에 필요한 최소한의 권한만 사용합니다. 현재 버전 기준:

  • 네트워크 접근 권한 없음 (원격 서버 연동, 원격 분석 기능 미사용)

  • (선택적으로 도입 가능) 진동/사운드 알림 기능 사용 시, 기기 진동/소리 기능에 접근할 수 있습니다.

Introduction

The Billiards Shot Timer app (the “App”) provides a shot clock and simple score tracking for billiards games (including 3-cushion, 4-ball, and pocket billiards). The current version does not use any third-party SDKs and runs its timer and score features locally on the device.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

  • No collection of personal identifiers (accounts, emails, real names, etc.).
  • In-app data such as scores, innings, player names, and settings are used locally on your device only and are not transmitted to external servers.
  • No analytics or logging is sent to remote servers (no online tracking).

Permissions

The App uses only minimal permissions necessary for its features. In the current release:

  • No network permissions are required for core functionality (no remote server integration, no remote analytics).
  • (Optionally, if implemented) WAKE_LOCK: May be used to keep the screen on during matches when a “keep screen awake” option is provided.
  • (Optionally, if implemented) Vibration/Sound: May use the device’s vibration and sound features to provide shot-completion alerts.

Device Features

  • The shot timer relies on the device clock for timing accuracy.
  • Optional features (if enabled in future updates) may include:
    • Keep screen on during active matches.
    • Vibration/sound cues when time is about to expire or has expired.

Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, such as advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or authentication providers.

Advertising / Monetization

This release of the App does not display ads and does not offer in-app purchases. If we introduce ads, analytics, or other monetization features in the future, we will update this Policy, add references to the relevant SDK policies, and obtain any required user consent where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

Security

  • The App does not transmit user data to external servers; most data stays on your device.
  • We recommend that you follow standard mobile security practices, such as setting a device screen lock and keeping your operating system and security patches up to date.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in laws, regulations, or App features. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. For material changes, we may also provide additional notice in the App or via store release notes.
