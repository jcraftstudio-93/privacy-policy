Privacy Policy / 개인정보처리방침 QR Scan Log

This Privacy Policy explains how the QR Scan Log app (“App”) collects, uses, and shares information. Developer/contact details are provided on the Google Play Store listing (“Developer” section).

1) Summary

  • The App stores your scan history and favorites locally on your device (on-device storage).
  • The App does not require account creation.
  • The App may show ads via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Ad services may collect data as described below.
  • If you choose to open a scanned link, your device may connect to that website.

2) Information You Provide

The App may store the following on your device:

  • Scanned content (e.g., a URL or text value)
  • Scan time (timestamp)
  • Favorite status
  • Optional short memo/tag you enter
  • App settings (e.g., language selection, “confirm before opening links”)

The developer does not receive this information directly from the App.

3) Automatically Collected Information

The App itself does not intentionally collect personal information to the developer’s servers. However, third-party services used by the App (such as advertising) may collect certain information automatically:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, and coarse location derived from IP
  • Ad measurement data (impressions/clicks) and related diagnostics
  • Approximate device information (OS version, device model, language)

4) Advertising (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob)

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to provide and measure ads, and (depending on your settings and region) personalize advertising.

Learn more: Google Ads & technologies and Google Privacy Policy.

5) Opening Links / External Websites

When you open a scanned URL in an external browser:

  • Your device connects to the destination website directly.
  • The destination website’s privacy policy and data practices apply.

The App may display a heuristic “domain safety” label. This is informational only and not a security guarantee.

6) Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device. The App may limit the number of stored items (e.g., by removing the oldest item when storage reaches a cap). You can delete items or clear all saved records within the App. Uninstalling the App typically removes local data.

7) Sharing of Information

The developer does not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only in these cases:

  • Advertising providers (e.g., Google Mobile Ads/AdMob) to serve and measure ads
  • Legal requirements if required by law or to protect rights and safety

8) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum required age in your country), and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9) Security

The App stores data locally on your device. While reasonable measures are taken, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

10) Your Choices

  • Clear saved history/favorites from within the App.
  • Disable ad personalization through your Google and device settings (where available).
  • Choose whether to open scanned links and whether to enable confirmation prompts.

11) Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be available at the URL where this policy is hosted. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12) Contact

For developer contact information, please refer to the Google Play Store listing page for this App (Developer section).
