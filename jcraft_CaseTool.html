1) Overview

This app helps you convert text case (UPPER/lower/Title/Sentence/Invert), keep History and Favorites, and optionally unlock limits for 24 hours by watching a rewarded ad. We designed the app to minimize data collection and to process text locally on your device whenever possible.

2) Data we process

Text you enter

  • Text you input and converted results are processed locally on your device.
  • No login is required and we do not ask for your name, email, or phone number inside the app.

History & Favorites (stored on device)

  • The app stores recent conversion records on your device (local storage).
  • Default limits: Favorites up to 30, History up to 50. When exceeded, the oldest items are removed automatically.
  • If you unlock limits via rewarded ad, the app stores an unlock expiration time on device (valid for 24 hours).

App settings (stored on device)

  • Theme preference (dark mode on/off)
  • Language preference (if you change it manually)
  • Unlock expiration time (if you unlock via rewarded ad)

3) Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads using Google AdMob (banner ads and rewarded ads). AdMob may collect or receive certain information to provide ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. This information may include device identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, depending on your device settings and region.

For details about how Google uses information from apps that use its services, please refer to Google’s AdMob/Google Ads policies and documentation.

4) Data sharing

  • Local app data (your text, History/Favorites) is stored on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Ad-related data may be processed by Google (AdMob) to serve ads and measure performance.

5) Data retention

  • History/Favorites and settings remain on your device until you delete them (e.g., clear app data or uninstall the app).
  • Unlock status expires automatically after 24 hours (stored as an on-device expiration timestamp).

6) Security

We use on-device storage for core features. While we strive to keep the app secure, no method of storage is 100% secure. You can remove stored data by clearing the app’s storage in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

7) Children’s privacy

This app is a general utility app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please remove app data from the device and refer to Google Play listing for developer contact.

8) Your choices

  • You may clear History/Favorites or clear app data at any time.
  • You can manage ad personalization through your device or Google settings (when available).
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted on this page.

10) Contact
