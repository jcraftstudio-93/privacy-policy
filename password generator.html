Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Password Generator app (“the App”) handles your information. The App generates passwords offline and provides Basic/Advanced modes, presets, policy templates, and optional clipboard auto-clear.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

  • No collection of identifiers such as accounts, emails, or names.
  • No collection of content like the generated password values.
  • No analytics/logging/remote transmission — the App works offline.

Clipboard

When you press “Copy,” the App writes the generated password to your device’s system clipboard. If enabled, it will attempt to clear the clipboard after a delay.

  • Auto-clear may fail due to OS/browser restrictions.
  • For sensitive data, consider clearing the clipboard immediately or using a password manager.

Permissions

The App uses only minimal permissions. The current version operates offline without network access. Clipboard access occurs only upon your explicit action (Copy button).

Third-Party Services

The current version does not include third-party SDKs (analytics, crash reporting, authentication, etc.).

Advertising / Monetization

No ads are shown in the current release. If we introduce ads or analytics in the future, we will update this Policy and include links to the relevant SDK policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

  • The App uses the platform’s secure random generator when available.
  • We recommend using two-factor authentication and a password manager for important accounts.
